About me

Computer Science Engineer (2019) and a Master on Natura Language Analyzing and Processing (2020) by the University of the Basque Country (UPV/EHU). Right now, I am a PhD Student on Natural Language Processing at the University of the Basque Country (UPV/EHU). Part of the IXA research group (ixa.si.ehu.eus, 2018-), group A by the Basque Goverment. I received the research Ikasiker grant (2018-2019) and the Basque Goverment predoctoral grant (2020-present). I participated on 3 R&D project founded through a competitive call by the Basque Goverment. I have several publications (see Publications section) on international conferences, which some of them are on top conferences (SCIE 1). My research focuses on Artificial Intelligence and Language Technologies, more precisely, I am interested on efficient methods on low resource Information Extraction.
