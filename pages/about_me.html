About me

I was born in Donosti, a beautiful city and the capital of Gipuzkoa, Basque Country. I studied Computer Science bachelor and the Language Analyzing and Processing master at the University of the Basque Country (UPV/EHU). Meanwhile, I worked as an internship on IXA group, where I developed my bachelor and masters thesis, both related to Relation Extraction and data-augmentation techniques. Both thesis were accepted on international conferences, the first one on LREC2020 and the second on IberLEF2020.

On my free time I like to watch series and movies, if possible in original audio. Actually I speak natively Spanish and Basque, I do my best effort with English and also I'm learning Japanese in a self-taught way. That's why one of the plans I have in mind is to travel to Japan and learn about their culture.
