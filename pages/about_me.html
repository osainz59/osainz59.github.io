About me

I was born in Donosti, a beautiful city and the capital of Gipuzkoa, Basque Country. I studied Computer Science bachelor and the Language Analyzing and Processing master at the University of the Basque Country (UPV/EHU). Meanwhile, I worked as an internship on IXA group, where I developed my bachelor and masters thesis, both related to Relation Extraction and data-augmentation techniques. Both thesis were accepted on international conferences, the first one on LREC2020 and the second on IberLEF2020. Actually im doing a Ph.D on low-resource Information Extraction at IXA Research group.

As my area of intereset is the Artificial Inteligence I have some years of experience on traditional Machine Learning and Deep Learning.
